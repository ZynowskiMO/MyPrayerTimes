## Summary (one line)

Islamic prayer times, calculated locally in-game for European cities — with
reminders, calculation methods, and a clean setup wizard.

## Description

**MyPrayerTimes** shows the five daily Islamic prayer times (plus sunrise) for your
location, computed right inside the game — no internet connection needed.

- Five daily prayers + sunrise, with a live countdown and the next prayer
  highlighted.
- Pick from a built-in list of European cities, or add your own location
  (coordinates + UTC offset, optional EU daylight saving).
- All standard calculation methods (Muslim World League, Egyptian, Karachi,
  Umm al-Qura, ISNA, Dubai, Kuwait, Qatar, Singapore, Tehran, Turkey, …) and the
  Asr school (Standard / Hanafi).
- Reminders: a configurable alert before each prayer, an at-time alert, and an
  optional sound.
- Movable, lockable, minimisable window; a first-run wizard sets everything up.

**Accuracy:** prayer-time math is ported from adhan-js (Batoul Apps) and verified
to within ±1 minute. Calculated times are an aid — please confirm against a
trusted local source, especially at high latitudes.

Works on Retail and TBC Classic. Open source (MIT).
